# Business Owner Guide

## 1. Create your account

- Go to `/auth/register`
- Enter your business name, email, and password
- Check your inbox to verify your email

## 2. Log in

- Go to `/admin`
- Sign in with your email and password

## 3. Add categories

- Open **Categories** from the sidebar
- Click **Add category**
- Provide a name and optional display order
- Use **Translations** to add localized names

## 4. Add products

- Open **Products** from the sidebar
- Click **Add product**
- Provide name, price, category, and optional image URL
- Use **Translations** to localize name and description
- Use bulk actions to delete or activate/deactivate products

## 5. Customize your QR code

- Open **QR Code** from the sidebar
- Download SVG/PNG/PDF files
- Update colors or embed a logo
- Use the **Test QR Code** button to preview the menu

## 6. Update your slug

- In **QR Code**, update your business slug
- The QR code regenerates automatically
- Your menu URL becomes `/menu/{slug}`

## Tips

- Keep product names short for easy scanning
- Add translations for your most common languages
- Use clear category ordering for faster navigation
